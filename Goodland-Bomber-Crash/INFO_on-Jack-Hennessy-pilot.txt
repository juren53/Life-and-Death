Bio: WWII Veteran and Aerial Firefighter
John Francis "Jack" Hennessy, RTD arriving in UK 3/22/1944. Jack survived the war and went onto become an aerial firefighter in Los Angeles. On 17 Sep 1966, Hennessy took off from the Lockheed Air Terminal in North Hollywood, to fight an LA county fire in the area. A turbojet on his retrofitted tanker AJ-1 Savage, flamed out, causing the plane to stall on takeoff. It crashed as Jack attempted to force a landing of the heavy aircraft. He did not survive the impact, he was 47 years old. Source: Facebook.
